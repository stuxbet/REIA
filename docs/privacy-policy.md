## What the app accesses

- **Location (while using the app):** to drop a pin at your current position and
  reverse‑geocode it to a street address while scouting. Requested only when you
  use the capture screen. Never accessed in the background.
- **Photos / camera:** to attach photos to properties you log.
- **Property addresses you capture or enter:** used to look up public records.

## What's stored, and where

- **On your device:** saved deals, leads, photos, notes, and settings live in a
  local database on your phone. They are not uploaded anywhere unless you trigger
  one of the actions below.
- **On the backend you operate:** when you explicitly tap **"Pull RentCast
  Intel,"** the app sends that property's **address** to your self‑hosted backend,
  which requests public property/owner/tax records from RentCast and returns them
  to your device. The backend holds the RentCast API key; it is not in the app.

## Third‑party services

- **RentCast** (property data) — receives only the property **address** you choose
  to enrich, only when you tap to enrich that specific property, relayed via your
  backend. See RentCast's own privacy policy.
- No analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs are used.

## Outreach

When you contact a property owner, the app **composes** a message and hands it off
to your own Mail, Messages, or Phone app. Nothing is sent through our servers —
**you are the sender**, and your normal carrier/email terms apply.

## Data sharing & sale

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or data
brokers. The only outbound data is the address you choose to enrich.

## Data retention & deletion

Your data stays on your device until you delete it (remove a deal/lead, or
uninstall the app, which removes the local database). Enrichment lookups are not
retained beyond fulfilling the request [unless you, the operator, enable caching
on the backend].

## Children

REIA is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data
from them.
